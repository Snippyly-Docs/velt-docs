There are three components associated with `Notifications` in the Velt SDK:
- `VeltNotificationsTool` - A button to open the `Notifications Panel`
- `VeltNotificationsPanel` - A panel that shows current `Notifications`
- `VeltNotificationsHistoryPanel` - A side panel that shows a history of all `Notifications`
